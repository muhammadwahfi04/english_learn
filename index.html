📝 6. To Be — am / is / are

"To Be" adalah kata kerja paling dasar dalam bahasa Inggris, artinya "adalah/sedang".

Rumus dasar

  • I → am | I am a student.
  • He / She / It → is | She is happy.
  • You / We / They → are | They are friends.

Kalimat negatif — tambahkan "not"

I am not (I'm not) tired. She is not (isn't) here. They are not (aren't) students.

Kalimat tanya — pindahkan To Be ke depan

Am I late? — Yes, you are. Is she a teacher? — Yes, she is. Are they happy? — No, they aren't.

Tahap 2 — Elementary A1–A2

Tenses, question words, prepositions, adjectives, dan percakapan sehari-hari

⏰ 1. Simple Present Tense

Digunakan untuk kebiasaan, fakta umum, dan rutinitas sehari-hari. Bayangkan kamu sedang cerita ke teman tentang apa yang kamu lakukan tiap hari — itulah Simple Present.
🎯 Kapan dipakai?
🔁
Kebiasaan rutin
I wake up at 6 AM every day.
🌍
Fakta umum
The earth moves around the sun.
❤️
Kesukaan/pendapat
I love spicy food.
📅
Jadwal tetap
The bus leaves at 7 AM.
✅ Positif
I / You / We / They + V1
He / She / It + V1 + s/es
I study English every night. She works at a hospital.
❌ Negatif
I / You / We / They + don't + V1
He / She / It + doesn't + V1
I don't eat meat. He doesn't like coffee.
❓ Tanya
Do + I/you/we/they + V1?
Does + he/she/it + V1?
Do you like music? — Yes, I do. Does she drive? — No, she doesn't.
💡 Tips: Kapan tambah -s atau -es?
Biasa → tambah -s
eat → eats, work → works
Akhiran -s, -sh, -ch, -x, -o → tambah -es
wash → washes, go → goes
Akhiran konsonan + y → -ies
study → studies, try → tries
Akhiran vokal + y → -s biasa
play → plays, say → says
💬 Dialog — Cerita rutinitas pagi
Rina: What time do you usually wake up?
Budi: I wake up at 5 AM. Then I exercise for 30 minutes.
Rina: Wow! Does your sister wake up early too?
Budi: No, she doesn't. She wakes up at 7 AM.

🔄 2. Present Continuous Tense

Digunakan untuk aktivitas yang sedang terjadi sekarang atau sementara waktu ini. Bayangkan kamu sedang video call dan menjelaskan apa yang sedang kamu lakukan.
🎯 Kapan dipakai?
▶️
Sedang berlangsung
I am eating right now.
⏳
Sementara waktu ini
She is living with her aunt this month.
📱
Ngomongin di WA/chat
"Lagi ngapain?" → What are you doing?
🚫
Perilaku yang menjengkelkan
He is always interrupting me!
✅ Positif
I + am + V-ing
He / She / It + is + V-ing
You / We / They + are + V-ing
I am watching a movie. She is cooking in the kitchen. They are playing outside.
❌ Negatif
I am not sleeping now. He isn't working today. We aren't going anywhere.
❓ Tanya
Are you eating? — Yes, I am. Is she sleeping? — No, she isn't. What are you doing? — I'm studying.
💡 Cara membuat V-ing yang benar
Biasa → + ing
eat → eating, sleep → sleeping
Akhiran -e → hilangkan e, + ing
write → writing, come → coming
KVK pendek → dobel konsonan + ing
run → running, sit → sitting
Akhiran -ie → ganti jadi -ying
lie → lying, die → dying
🔍 Bedanya Simple Present vs Present Continuous
Simple Present
I eat rice every day. (kebiasaan)
She works at a bank. (pekerjaan tetap)
He plays football on Sundays. (rutin)
Present Continuous
I am eating right now. (sedang makan)
She is working late tonight. (sedang kerja)
He is playing football now. (sedang main)
💬 Dialog — Pesan WhatsApp
Ani: Hey, where are you? What are you doing?
Doni: I'm sitting at a café. I'm waiting for my friend.
Ani: Is it raining there? It's raining here.
Doni: No, it's sunny here. The sun is shining brightly!

⏪ 3. Simple Past Tense

Digunakan untuk kejadian yang sudah selesai di masa lalu. Ada kata petunjuk waktu yang sering muncul: yesterday, last week, last night, ago, in 2020, dll.
✅ Positif
Subject + V2 (bentuk lampau)
I watched a movie last night. She went to Bali last week. They ate nasi goreng for breakfast.
❌ Negatif
Subject + didn't + V1
I didn't go to school yesterday. He didn't call me last night.
❓ Tanya
Did + subject + V1?
Did you eat breakfast? — Yes, I did. Did she call? — No, she didn't.

💬 Dialog — Cerita liburan kemarin

❓ 4. Question Words (Kata Tanya)

Kata tanya dipakai di awal kalimat untuk menggali informasi. Setiap kata tanya punya fungsi berbeda — jangan tertukar!
Kata TanyaArtiContoh
WhatApaWhat is your job? — I'm a teacher.
WhereDi manaWhere do you live? — In Palembang.
WhenKapanWhen is your birthday? — July 10th.
WhoSiapaWho is that? — That's my teacher.
WhyKenapaWhy are you sad? — Because I'm tired.
HowBagaimanaHow are you? — I'm fine, thanks.
How manyBerapa banyakHow many siblings do you have? — Two.
How muchBerapa hargaHow much is this? — Rp 50.000.
WhichYang manaWhich do you prefer, tea or coffee?
WhoseMilik siapaWhose bag is this? — It's mine.
How oldBerapa umurHow old are you? — I'm 17.
How farBerapa jauhHow far is your school? — 2 km.
How oftenSeberapa seringHow often do you exercise? — Twice a week.
What timeJam berapaWhat time does school start? — At 7.
💡 How many vs How much?
How many → benda yang bisa dihitung
How many books? / How many friends?
How much → harga atau benda tak terhitung
How much money? / How much water?
💬 Dialog — Kenalan pertama kali
Maya: Hi! What's your name?
Andi: I'm Andi. And you?
Maya: I'm Maya. Where are you from?
Andi: I'm from Palembang. How about you?
Maya: Me too! How old are you?
Andi: I'm 18. Why do you ask?
Maya: Because we might go to the same school!

📍 5. Prepositions (Kata Depan)

Prepositions menunjukkan hubungan posisi, waktu, atau arah antara suatu benda/orang dengan benda/orang lain. Tiga yang paling sering bikin bingung: in, on, at.
📌 in / on / at — Panduan lengkap
IN
Tempat:
in a room, in a city, in Indonesia
in the box, in the car, in the sky
Waktu:
in the morning/afternoon/evening
in January, in 2024, in summer
ON
Tempat:
on the table, on the wall, on the floor
on the street, on the bus
Waktu:
on Monday, on my birthday
on 17 August, on weekends
AT
Tempat:
at school, at the airport, at home
at the door, at the bus stop
Waktu:
at 7 o'clock, at noon, at midnight
at night, at the weekend (British)

💬 Dialog — Mencari lokasi di mall
Rika: Excuse me, where is the restroom?
Guard: It's on the second floor, next to the elevator.
Rika: And the food court? Is it far from here?
Guard: No, it's right behind you. It's between the cinema and the bookstore.
Rika: Oh! I see it. Thank you so much!

✨ 6. Adjectives (Kata Sifat)

Adjectives dipakai untuk mendeskripsikan orang, benda, atau suasana. Posisinya biasanya sebelum noun atau setelah to be.
Sebelum noun (kata benda)
She has a beautiful smile. It was a boring movie. I need a big bag.
Setelah to be
The food is delicious. He looks tired today. I feel happy.
