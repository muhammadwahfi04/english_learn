📝 6. To Be — am / is / are

"To Be" adalah kata kerja paling dasar dalam bahasa Inggris, artinya "adalah/sedang".

Rumus dasar

  • I → am | I am a student.
  • He / She / It → is | She is happy.
  • You / We / They → are | They are friends.

Kalimat negatif — tambahkan "not"

I am not (I'm not) tired. She is not (isn't) here. They are not (aren't) students.

Kalimat tanya — pindahkan To Be ke depan

Am I late? — Yes, you are. Is she a teacher? — Yes, she is. Are they happy? — No, they aren't.

Tahap 2 — Elementary A1–A2

Tenses, question words, prepositions, adjectives, dan percakapan sehari-hari

⏰ 1. Simple Present Tense

Digunakan untuk kebiasaan, fakta, dan rutinitas sehari-hari.

Rumus kalimat positif

I/You/We/They + V1 | I eat rice every day. He/She/It + V1+s/es | She drinks coffee.

Rumus negatif

I/You/We/They + don't + V1 | I don't like fish. He/She/It + doesn't + V1 | He doesn't play football.

Rumus kalimat tanya

Do you like music? — Yes, I do. / No, I don't. Does she work here? — Yes, she does. / No, she doesn't.

🔄 2. Present Continuous Tense

Digunakan untuk aktivitas yang sedang terjadi saat ini.

Rumus: Subject + am/is/are + V-ing

I am eating now. (Saya sedang makan sekarang) She is studying English. (Dia sedang belajar Inggris) They are playing football. (Mereka sedang bermain bola)

Cara menambahkan -ing

  • Biasa: eat → eating, sleep → sleeping
  • Akhiran e: write → writing, make → making
  • Konsonan ganda: sit → sitting, run → running

⏪ 3. Simple Past Tense

Digunakan untuk kejadian yang sudah terjadi di masa lalu.

Regular Verbs — tambahkan -ed

play → played | I played football yesterday. walk → walked | She walked to school. study → studied | He studied last night.
